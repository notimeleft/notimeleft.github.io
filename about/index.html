A couple words to describe me: Student, Creative, Worrywort, Gritty. I'm really interested in software that forms a connection between user and machine, and it is totally a cliche and a buzzword-y turn of phrase to say this but I believe that someday apps will make us far better artists, scientists, builders, cooks, writers, and runners than we would be capable of without them. What else matters in life?!

These days I'm learning apple's UIKit and iOS frameworks, and iterating obsessively over my current app, SongBrush. I graduated from Brandeis with a degree in computer science in 2017 and took a year to decide what realm of software to devote myself to. It was then that I discovered Swift and I latched onto the notion that a single person can make a mark on the world with app development. It'll probably be an extremely small mark, one that barely registers in the vast ocean of Apple's app store, but there it is: the chance to build my own dreams.

In the meantime, I'm excited to be exploring cutting-edge mobile tech and sharing it with those passersby who stumble on my humble projects. It'll be a long journey to enjoy, I hope.
